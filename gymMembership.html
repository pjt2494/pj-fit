Gym Membership



FORGET CROWDED GYMS AND FIGHTING FOR EQUIPMENT


Our gym membership gives you access to our 5800 square foot state-of-the-art fitness facility, featuring tons of floor space and top of the line equipment from Rogue, Atlantis, Hammer Strength and Eleiko..

Whether you are following one of our signature programs, a personalized program from one of our coaches, or your own program, our facility has everything you need to build strength and functional fitness in a safe, spacious and inspiring setting.

But Suazo Fit is so much more than a gym. It’s a community of supportive and like-minded strength seekers – that strive to make positive life changes through functional fitness. It’s a place where people come to train with intention and crush their goals.
